# Photography Exposure Basics

A tutor that teaches the exposure triangle — ISO, aperture, and shutter speed — not as isolated definitions, but as interconnected decisions you make every time you pick up a camera.

**What it teaches:** How ISO, aperture, and shutter speed work individually and together, how to choose settings for different lighting conditions and creative goals, camera shooting modes, and exposure compensation.

**What it demonstrates:** How ScaffoldAI handles an **applied skill** — settings that interact with each other, where understanding the relationships matters more than memorizing the numbers.

---

## Setup

1. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) and create a new Project.
2. Open the `tutor-instructions.md` file in this folder.
3. Copy the entire contents and paste it into your Project's **Custom Instructions**.
4. Start a new conversation within the project.

That's it. The tutor will guide you from there.

---

## Suggested First Prompts

Not sure where to start? Try one of these:

- **"I've been shooting on Auto mode and want to understand what's actually happening when I take a photo."** — Perfect for someone who owns a camera but hasn't ventured beyond Auto. The tutor will build from what you already know about taking pictures.

- **"I understand ISO and shutter speed, but aperture and f-stops confuse me."** — Tells the tutor exactly where to focus. It'll verify what you know, then zero in on the gap.

- **"I want to learn how to blur the background in my portraits."** — A creative goal the tutor can work backward from. It'll teach you the specific settings and the reasoning behind them.

- **"Give me a shooting scenario and I'll tell you what settings I'd use."** — Jumps to application. The tutor will present real-world situations and coach you through the decision-making process.
