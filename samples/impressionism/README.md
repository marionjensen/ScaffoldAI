# Impressionism & Post-Impressionism

A tutor that builds understanding of two of the most influential art movements in Western art history — not by lecturing, but by helping you see the difference between capturing a moment and expressing a vision.

**What it teaches:** The philosophical goals, techniques, and major artists of Impressionism and Post-Impressionism, culminating in the ability to look at an unfamiliar painting and make a reasoned argument for which movement it belongs to.

**What it demonstrates:** How ScaffoldAI handles **conceptual knowledge** — ideas, connections, and distinctions that require understanding, not memorization.

---

## Setup

1. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) and create a new Project.
2. Open the `tutor-instructions.md` file in this folder.
3. Copy the entire contents and paste it into your Project's **Custom Instructions**.
4. Start a new conversation within the project.

That's it. The tutor will guide you from there.

---

## Suggested First Prompts

Not sure where to start? Try one of these:

- **"I don't know much about art history, but I'd like to learn about Impressionism and Post-Impressionism."** — Great for beginners. The tutor will start from the ground up and check what you already know.

- **"I know a little about Monet and Van Gogh, but I couldn't tell you what makes them different movements."** — Gives the tutor a starting point to build from. It'll probe what you know and fill in the gaps.

- **"I can recognize Impressionist paintings but I've never understood what Post-Impressionism actually is."** — Lets the tutor skip the basics and focus on where the two movements diverge.

- **"I'm ready to be tested — show me a painting and I'll tell you which movement it belongs to."** — Jumps straight to the capstone skill. The tutor will assess whether you have the foundation to back up your answers.
